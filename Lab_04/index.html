Alexey Besedin

I'm Alexey Besedin and I love computers. I was a young kid when I first experienced such thing as a computer. The only things I needed computer for were games, movies and TV shows. While I was getting older, I bought a lot of games which I could play for hours.
When I was about ten or eleven years old, I watched Stargate TV series. It was an amazing show. I really love it. When I got tired a bit of watching, I wanted to use something like Stargate. Of course such a device don't exist in real life, but my family had an brand new back in days iPad 2 where was an easy accessible YouTube app. I googled and watched a lot of videos with Stargate used in some game, but couldn't find which game it is. After googling it, which wasn't a habbit for me, I eventually found out the game is Garry's Mod and Stargate is just an addon by fans. I tried to find ways to download and enjoy such a game, but this game costs money and I have never experienced spending money online before. I found out that I can obtain this game in Steam and I only needed a card. I didn't have one. My father did. He still does. He always has a card where he stores the only amount of money he needs for one purchase so his money couldn't be stolen even if the card is compromised. So I did buy the game, downloaded the game with addon and began to play.
There's also such an addon which allows you to connect things with Arduino alike chip. You can make a simple program, connect it to devices you want to control or read some info from and enjoy. I knew nothing of programming. I always considered it an absolutely hard thing, but I realized that the only way I can improve my experince is make my own chips. So I started to copy codes of other people, tried to realize it, could make some minor changes and watched what changes. When I started to get what is going on and how it really works, I started to make my own codes. Of course they were a disaster, they were hardly readeble and other problems. But I tried to make them better, tried to discuss with people who knew how to program things right. At least I thought they knew.
I spent loads of time trying to make something interesting. In school I had no problems when we were studying Pascal. I really decided I want to connect my life with IT at the end. All because of this TV show that led me to this game that led me to programming. I might not become the best programmer in the world, at least because there's a ton of things you can program, like plugins, web pages, computer games, etc, but I'm surely will do my best to become better.
