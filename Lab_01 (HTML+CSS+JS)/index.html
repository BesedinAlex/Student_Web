Explore Bustling Miami, FL

Miami is a huge city, it's impossible to see and do all of the popular activities in just a few days. Renting a Miami charter bus from GoGo Charters will certainly help, and so will a well-planned itinerary. Here are some of the attractions and activities you should include:

Beaches: Of course, everyone comes here for the beaches. Miami is proud of its strips of soft sand, equipped with everything you might need for a fabulous beach day. The number one beach in the city is Miami Beach, situated across Biscayne Bay on a barrier reef. South Beach is also a very popular destination, situated along the coast of Florida. The weather in Miami makes every day the perfect beach day.

Museums and Landmarks: Not many tourists come here for the museums but once they arrive here, the curiosity pushes them towards museums such as Vizcaya Museum and Gardens, an estate built in a European style, where visitors can admire art and furnishings, and the Lowe Art Museum,, a museum dedicated to art, ceramics and pottery dating from ancient times to present. Miami is also home to an ancient Spanish Monastery, built in Segovia, Spain in 1141, brought and assembled here in 1954.

Outdoor Fun: The beaches are the number one setting for outdoor fun, with many nightclubs and bars inviting visitors to dance on the sand, but you can also visit the Oleta River State Recreation Park, ideal for biking, canoeing, kayaking and other fun sports that can be played outdoors, and Jungle Island, a tropical garden that presents animal shows and exhibits. The Matheson Hammock Marina is a beautiful park where you can discover a man-made atoll pool. If you want to admire impressive architecture you can also visit Star Island, an artificial island where many celebrities live.

Day Trips: Groups who come to Miami usually choose to rent a Miami charter bus for their sightseeing trips to  Orlando, St. Petersburg, Tampa, Jacksonville and Tallahassee.
